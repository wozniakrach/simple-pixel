About Simple Pixel

Simple pixel was in part inspired by the lovely Hannah Zahn, who creates pixel art of a much higher quality than this site is capable of :) You can check out her art here.

Other resources used include:

  • html2canvas: supports screenshooting canvas for image download
  • GitHub pages: host for site
  • Google Fonts: source for font used ('VT323')
  • w3schools: used for help with creating elements like carousel

This site was created as a final project for CS290 at OSU. Thank you to all of the instructors and ULAs!
